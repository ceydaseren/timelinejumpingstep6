Step 6 Selection Phase

Establish the Emotional Signature

Your new timeline has a feeling. Three things happen here: you tune into what this reality actually feels like in your body, you check whether it is soul-driven or ego-driven, and you give your timeline a name you can return to again and again.

Action Steps
What you are completing in Step 6
1
Visualize your new timeline and write down the feelings that arise. Aim for descriptive, body-level words rather than concepts.
2
Run your vision through the soul aligned filter. Mark whether it feels soul driven or ego driven, and note what you discover.
3
Give your timeline a name. Keep it simple and direct. This name will carry you through the rest of the framework.
The feeling is the fuel

You have likely heard this in manifestation teachings before. The feeling you attach to a vision is the signal that broadcasts your chosen timeline outward. When you can genuinely access the feeling of a reality before it has physically arrived, you begin closing the gap between where you are and where you are going.

So as you hold your new timeline in your awareness, notice what arises. Does it feel expansive? Does it feel like your life force energy is increasing? Does it feel safe, empowering, joyful, easy and in flow? There will likely be more than one feeling. Let them all come through. The more vividly you can access this emotional state, the more powerfully it anchors your selection and sets the activation process in motion.

Before You Write
Close your eyes for a moment. Bring your new timeline fully into your awareness. Let yourself see it, sense it, and breathe into it. When a feeling stirs, open your eyes and write.

The soul aligned filter

This is a quick internal check, not a judgment. Ego-driven visions are usually about acquiring things that signal status or worth to the outside world. Soul-aligned visions expand you inward as much as outward. They tend to feel like a calling rather than a craving.

Money is worth examining here because it can go either way. If money appears in your new timeline, get curious about why it is there. Is it because you want to show the world what you have, or because it gives you the freedom to live your purpose and create meaningful experiences? The latter is soul-aligned. One check-in is all you need. Trust the first honest answer that comes.

Is this vision soul aligned or ego aligned?
Tune in honestly. Your answer shapes how you move forward. Either result is information, not a verdict.
Soul Aligned
Feels like expansion, purpose, inner calling. It is about living fully, not proving anything.
Ego Aligned
Feels like acquiring, impressing, or filling a gap. Worth examining before moving on.
Name your timeline

Give your timeline a short, plain-language name. This is how you will refer to it in your mind, in your journal, and out loud. It should feel immediately recognizable to you, like the title of the chapter you are stepping into.

Your Timeline Name
What is this timeline about?
Imagine someone asks you directly: what timeline are you on? Your answer, without overthinking it, is the name.
Example: "I am on the timeline of being fully compensated and supported by my spiritual business."
